Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is the process of removing dents from a vehicle without damaging the original paint finish. This prevents toxic chemicals and pollutants from leaking into the environment and contaminating our surface water, rivers and lakes, and underground water supplies. It also means fewer replacement panels will end up in landfills. PDR is an eco-friendly solution to repair your hail-damaged vehicle.
